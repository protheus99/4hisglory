Welcome to 4 His Glory Ministries

Encouraging, Equipping, and Empowering our Community through the Word of God

4 His Glory Ministries is an outreach ministry inspired to encourage, equip, and empower our community through the word of God. It is our goal that those who don't know God, will get to know Him as their personal Savior and friend. For those that do know Him, our goal is to offer continuous spiritual, emotional, and financial growth opportunities.

We continue the work of God through empowerment programs, community social awareness sessions and service projects, retreats, and conferences. We are delighted that God has allowed you to embark upon this ministry. Your support makes it possible for us to continue what God has ordained us to do.

For more information, please feel free to contact our administration.
