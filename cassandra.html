Called to Serve, Anointed to Lead

4 His Glory Ministries was birthed after Cassandra received a word from God. Through her calling, God has equipped her to lead this ministry and give all the glory to Him.

Cassandra is available to minister to groups of any size. For speaking engagement inquiries, please call 931-246-6671.

A Heart for Ministry

Outreach

A licensed and ordained minister and evangelist, Cassandra has been dedicated to outreach for over 20 years. She has served on community leadership boards, ministered to the incarcerated and the homeless, and organized retreats, workshops and empowerment conferences.

Visionary

Having worked in several areas of outreach and community, Cassandra was acknowledged as having a special call on her life. By answering that call and yielding to God, she has encouraged and empowered hundreds. Through her selfless transparency, she is able to connect with, relate to, and comfort those from many walks of life.

Survivor

Although every story of survival is different, Cassandra's victory over domestic violence is a story of strength, grace, and faith. She uses her personal experiences and trials to empower others to overcome what seem to be insurmountable obstacles.

Encouraging, empowering, and equipping the whole family!

Experience the Ministry

Join Pastor Cassandra Johnson and 4 His Glory Ministries as we continue to serve our community with the love of Christ. Through transparency, faith, and the power of God's word, lives are being transformed.
